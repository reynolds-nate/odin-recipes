Flan

Leche Flan is a Filipino desert.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Vanilla

Directions

  1. Seperate Egg yolks from whites
  2. Mix egg yolks, vanilla, condensed milk and evaporated milk together in a bowl.
  3. Mix to combine.
  4. Strain to remove air bubbles.
  5. Heat pan and caramelize sugar.
  6. Pour mixture over top of caramel slowly.
  7. Wrap pan with tin foil and steam to completion. Cool before serving.
